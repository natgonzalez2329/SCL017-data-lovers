Ricktastic Facts

Justin Roiland himself voices both Rick and Morty

Rick and Morty's dynamic is a representation of mental illness

The show was inspired by Roiland's vulgar take on Back to the Future

Rick and Morty’s intro and theme song are a parody mix of Doctor Who and Farscape

Each one of Rick’s characteristic burps is a real burp

When traveling in cars Rick never wears a seatbelt

"Pickle Rick" was inspired by from Breaking Bad

Rick spends most of his time drunk

Die, bury and replace the own corpses, true sci-fi
